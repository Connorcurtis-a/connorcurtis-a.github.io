The Poker Journey

Running it up from $0.10/$0.20 to $500/$1000 at the Wynn

The definition of running it up. From zero.

It started with $0.10/$0.20 games online. Small town Michigan casinos playing $1/$2, where competing at Bellagio's $5/$10 tables seemed like an impossible dream. But Connor didn't just dream—he executed.

Through countless hours of grinding online, maintaining strict bankroll management, and staying disciplined through brutal downswings, Connor climbed the ranks. From micro-stakes to small stakes. From small stakes to mid-stakes. Each level conquered through skill, patience, and relentless improvement.

By 2021, Connor had turned professional—consistently profitable and ready for the big leagues. The move to Las Vegas in late 2023 was the final piece. Soon after, he found himself at the $10/$20 No-Limit Hold'em tables at the Wynn, competing against some of the world's best players.

Every day at noon sharp, the game would start. Battling elite players. Waiting for high-stakes whales to join. Managing six-figure swings with ice-cold composure. This was the dream realized—playing $500/$500/$1000 at the Salon in the Wynn, the highest public stakes game in Las Vegas.
